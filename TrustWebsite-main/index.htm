    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is TRUSTCOIN?
    TRUSTCOIN is a global, new generation crowdfunding platform and the first ever smart contract of its kind in the blockchain of BSC Blockchain It is a software algorithm performing the function of a distribution of affiliate commissions between community members.

    How are commissions paid?
    All commissions are paid in USDT directly and instantly to the members’ personal USDT wallets. The smart contract never receives payments; therefore, you will never have to wait on a commission payment as it’s paid directly to you.
    What is the risk of joining TRUSTCOIN?
    TRUSTCOIN is risk-free! There is no company, no admin, it is a self-operating system located on the blockchain that cannot be changed or destroyed. The code of the smart contract is in the public domain. All payments go directly into your personal wallet, without any hidden fees and without third-party resources. You can feel safe knowing that TRUSTCOIN will not go away.
    What personal information is needed to open an account?
    No personal information is required to open an account. The only thing required to create your account is a USDT wallet of your choice.
    How long can TRUSTCOIN last?
    TRUSTCOIN is a Smart Contract on the BSC blockchain which is fully decentralized and connected to the internet. TRUSTCOIN will be in business for as long as the BSC blockchain and internet exists.
    When will be my TRUSTCOIN token unlocked?
    Whenever you will get a single income from autopool matrix from fourth level and if you sponsored minimun two useres, all the locked TRUSTCOIN token will be unlocked and you can transfer or sell TRUSTCOIN tokens.
    Do I ever lose the people I refer to TRUSTCOIN?
    In TRUSTCOIN, your entire downline including direct , indirect, overflow or spill over follows you at all times in every step of the matrix.You will pass up a commission to your upline if you are not active on a position that generates an earning.However, once you become active on that level, you will get paid for any further sales that take place from that point onwards.
    Is it compulsory to recruit to earn from the system?
    No. TRUSTCOIN platform is arranged in such a way that you willl receive Auto-fill matrix income passively However, to make a lot of money, doing nothing will not work. You must try and help people build their teams, and once they are auto pilot , you will earn passively. The more active you are initially will determine how quickly you will reach this auto- earning mode.
    Can my account ever be closed?
    No, once your account is created it can never be removed or destroyed.
    Is there a limit to the amount of USDT you can earn?
    You can genuinely earn UNLIMITED USDT in this business. The more people you refer and the more people you assist with growing their business, the higher your income potential becomes.
    How is the registration fee distrubuted in TRUSTCOIN?
    Smart contract distributes the registration fee in six parts:
    • Sponsor income 20% Distributed to sponsor, sponsor is the person who introduce TRUSTCOIN to new user.
    • Sub Admin income 20% Distributed to Sub Admin, Sub Admin is user who qualified exam and can handle team independently.
    • Global Autopool matrix 10% Distributed in autofill matrix,2% for each level upto 5 levels.
    • Level income for Sponsor 10% Distributed in sponsor structure, 1% for each level up to 10 levels.
    • Level income for Sub Admin 20% Distributed in Sub Admin structure, 2% for each level up to 10 levels.
    • Community & Project devlopment 20% reserved for project and community devlopment, which will be used to achieve road map targets.
    Who owns TRUSTCOIN?
    TRUSTCOIN platform is created with the public’s interest in mind and does not have managers or admins. This means that the platform is fully decentralized (i.e. it has no company, no leaders, or admins) and cannot be changed or altered at any time.
    What is a Smart Contract?
    A smart contract is nothing but a predetermined algorithm inside the blockchain which automates the execution of transactions.
    What is Decentralization?
    A system in which there are no admins, no servers, and no monitoring needed to manage the project. The creators of the platform are the same project participants like you.
    I don't have Referral link, can I register with TRUSTCOIN?
    No,You can not Register without referral or invitation, this is 100% refrral based system, because we are focusing on community building too not on investment only.
    What is the registration fee?
    Anyone can sign up on contract with 100 USDT.
